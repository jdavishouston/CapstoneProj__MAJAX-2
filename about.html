About Us

WE ARE NAACP

We are the home of grassroots activism for civil rights and social justice and equity.

We advocate, agitate, and litigate for the civil rights due to Black America.

In our cities, schools, companies, and courtrooms, we are the legacy of W.E.B. Du Bois, Ida B. Wells, Thurgood Marshall, and many other giants.

THRIVE: IN MOVEMENT. IN CULTURE. IN COMMUNITY.

OUR VISION

We envision an inclusive and diverse community rooted in liberation where all individuals can exercise their civil and human rights without discrimination. We are committed to building a world without racism where Black individuals can enjoy equitable opportunities in thriving communities.

OUR LEADERS

Our National Board and executive staff strongly believe in our mission and bring invaluable skills and experience in advocacy, organizing, and litigation to help push our efforts to secure civil rights and social justice and equity for all individuals.

WE MAKE AN IMPACT COLLECTIVELY

In our advocacy work, we ensure that Black lives and voices are seen and heard, demands are met, and that Black excellence is amplified. From classrooms and courtrooms to city halls and Congress, our dedicated network of members across the country works to secure the social and political power that will end race-based discrimination.
